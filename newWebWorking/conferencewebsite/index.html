About SMIC 2016

"SZABIST Multidisciplinary International Conference 2016” (SMIC 2016) at SZABIST Dubai Campus, United Arab Emirates, on January 09, 2016. The theme of the event is “Contemporary Issues & Trends”.

Learn More

Organizers

The Shaheed Zulfikar Ali Bhutto Institute of Science and Technology (SZABIST) is a vibrant and leading Pakistani university providing world class education. SZABIST maintains its commitment to exceptional undergraduate and graduate instruction. SZABIST has 4 campuses

An Intellectual forum to invite s related, but not limited, to the contemporary trends and issues in the fields of Computer Science, Management Science and Media Science.
