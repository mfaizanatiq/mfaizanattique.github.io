ADPIEC, the worlds’ most influential oil & gas exhibition and conference came to a closing on the 12th of November in the capital Abu Dhabi. UTS is proud to be one of the key UAE companies that took part in the exhibition, with large presence in Hall 1.

This year, ADIPEC was held under the theme, innovation and sustainability in a new energy world.

The event was the largest to date, bringing together over 2,000 exhibiting companies, including the world's leading national and international firms, 600 speakers, 7,000 delegates and 95,000 attendees from more than 120 countries.

Some of UTS’s key principles and partners that were showcased this year included Carrier, Sumitomo Corporation, MECO, HPI, Egersund Group, Hoover Container Solutions, PCS Italiana & PCS Cables.
